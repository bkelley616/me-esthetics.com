About Me:

Hi everyone! My name is Morgan and I have recently graduated from the Brown Aveda institute for esthetics. I am so excited to jump right into the esthetics world and build my portfolio. I live in Broadview Heights and love to spend time with my dog, Winston, in my free time.

As a licensed esthetician, I am certified in microdermabrasion, customized facials, facial waxing, body waxing, body wrapping, relaxation massage, electrotherapy, brow tinting and lamination, lash lifting and tinting, and makeup application.
